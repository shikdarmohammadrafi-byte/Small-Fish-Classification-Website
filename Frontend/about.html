About FishAI

Making fish identification accessible through artificial intelligence and machine learning

Our Mission

FishAI was created with a simple but powerful mission: to make fish identification accessible to everyone, from marine biologists and conservationists to aquarium hobbyists and curious beachgoers.

We believe that understanding and appreciating aquatic life should not require years of specialized training. By leveraging cutting-edge artificial intelligence, we're democratizing marine biology and fostering greater awareness of our planet's aquatic ecosystems.
