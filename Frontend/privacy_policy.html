Introduction

Important Notice:

This is a demonstration website created for educational purposes as part of a Bachelor Degree thesis. It is not a commercial service and should not be used for professional or commercial applications.

Information We Collect

Images You Upload

When you upload fish images for classification, we temporarily process these images using our AI model. Images are not permanently stored on our servers and are deleted immediately after processing.

Usage Data

We may collect anonymous usage statistics, such as the number of classifications performed and general usage patterns, to improve our service. This data does not identify individual users.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide fish classification services
  • To improve our AI model's accuracy and performance
  • To maintain and enhance our website functionality
  • To generate anonymous usage analytics

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. Images you upload are processed locally on our servers and are not shared with any external services or stored permanently.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the information we collect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights

As this is a demonstration service, we do not retain personal data. If you have any concerns about your privacy or our practices, please contact us using the information provided below.
